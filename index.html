NOREN was born from a simple question: why can't your favourite anime and the finest linen coexist in one garment?

Our Story

Founded in 2021 by two anime fans and a textile artist, NOREN started as a small experiment in a Bengaluru studio. We wondered if we could take the breathable magic of pure European flax linen and pair it with the vivid, emotional worlds of anime storytelling.

Three years and thousands of happy customers later, we're still that small, obsessive studio — just with a much bigger canvas. Every design goes through months of development with independent anime artists before a single thread is cut.

Linen is our commitment to the planet. Anime is our commitment to culture. Together, they make something truly unforgettable.

The Fabric

We use only Grade-A European flax linen sourced from sustainable farms in Belgium and France. Linen is one of the oldest and most sustainable textiles — it requires no irrigation, no pesticides, and every part of the plant is used.

Our linen is pre-washed for softness, then treated for colour-fastness before our artists' designs are applied using reactive dye printing — the most eco-conscious method available that still delivers vivid, lasting colour.

What does that mean for you? A shirt that breathes in Bengaluru's heat, feels better every season, and tells a story no one else is wearing.
